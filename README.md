# 🌍 LILLEGUD

*you are the sky. they are the dust.*

A tiny god game that runs entirely in your browser. You hover above a hand-painted island, drop a few souls onto the grass, and watch them build a civilization across generations — completely on their own.

> **🤖 Made by AI:** This game was designed, written, and tested by **Claude Fable 5** (Anthropic's AI model), based on ideas and direction from [@08Robfin](https://github.com/08Robfin). Every mechanic, sprite, and line in it came from the AI.

**▶️ Play it:** open `index.html` in any modern browser — no install, no internet, no dependencies. Works on desktop and mobile.

---

## ✨ What it is

LILLEGUD is a sandbox "ant farm with feelings." The simulation is 100% autonomous — your little people work, build, age, and die whether you help them or not. You're just the weather above their heads. Sometimes literally.

Every island is **procedurally generated**, so no two games look the same: ragged coastlines, beaches, forests, rocky highlands, and snow-capped peaks, all rendered in a cozy isometric diorama style with a full day/night cycle, drifting clouds, and chimney smoke.

## ⚡ Your divine powers

| Power | What it does |
|-------|--------------|
| 🖐️ **Look** | Pan, zoom, and inspect anyone or anything |
| 🧍 **Soul** | Breathe a human into the dust — this is how everything starts |
| 🦌 **Deer** | Release gentle wildlife onto the island |
| 🐺 **Wolf** | Release something hungry. The deer will notice |
| 🌲 **Tree** | Plant a tree anywhere there's soil |
| 🌧️ **Rain** | Call a raincloud — saplings sprout and forests thicken beneath it |
| ⚡ **Smite** | Lightning. Screen shake. Consequences |

## 🏘️ What the villagers do (all by themselves)

- **Chop wood** and physically haul the logs home on their backs
- **Build huts**, then farms, then upgrade crowded huts into two-story **Big Houses**
- **Plant new saplings** near the village when the forest runs low — they never deforest their own island
- **Progress through eras**: Empty Age → Wanderers → Camp → Hamlet → Village → Town
- **Raise families**: babies are born in houses, households rebalance, and the food supply naturally caps how big the town can get

## 🧬 Lifecycles & generations

Every person has a name, an age, and a lifespan:

- **👶 Children** are visibly tiny, toddle around near home, and grow up in real time
- **🧍 Adults** do all the heavy work
- **🧓 Elders** turn white-haired, walk slower, and spend their days strolling and planting trees
- Eventually everyone **passes peacefully**, leaving a small tombstone and a line in the chronicle — *"Sigrid passed peacefully at 71, full of years."*

Watch long enough and the founders you summoned will be gone, replaced by grandchildren they never met. 🥹

## 🔍 Inspect everything

Hover (or tap, on mobile) over any person or house:

- **People** show their name, life stage and exact age, what they're doing right now, what they're carrying, and where they live
- **Houses** show their type, occupants vs. capacity, upgrade progress, and a full resident list

## 🎮 Controls

- **Drag** to pan · **scroll / pinch** to zoom
- **Click** with a power selected to use it
- **Keys 1–7** switch powers quickly
- Event log (bottom left) tells the island's story as it happens
